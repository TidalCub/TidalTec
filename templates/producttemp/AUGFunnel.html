Description
A Funnel for AUG Hi-cap magazines to easily and quickly fill your magazines and get you back in the action. This is a continuation of the m4 Funnel, requested by a customer. This will work with most AUG hi-cap magazines.

With this design, there is no more wasting BBs trying to make a funnel with your hand, simply clip onto the mag meaning your hands are free to pour BBs and hold the mag in place, making it easier to fill. This is a great addition to any airsofter who runs an AUG. Available in a range of colours to suit any loadout. Don't have the colour you want? Get in touch and we will try to add a new colour! The BB funnel has been tried and tested.

This product is made using eco-friendly PLA, which is 3D printed. Polylactide (PLA), is a renewable thermoplastic and a polymer. It is processed from the starch of plants such as corn, sugar cane and sugar beet, making it environmentally friendly and sustainable. As this is a 3D product, there may be slight imperfections, such as visible lines. We ensure you that this has no effect on the product's performance. All products are inspected.
