Description
A Funnel for Hi-Cap m4 Mags to easily and quickly fill up your magazines and get you back into the game quicker. Great for large-capacity magazines, like drums mags, or even standard 300rnd Mags.

With this design, there is no more wasting BBs trying to make a funnel with your hand, simply clip onto the mag meaning your hands are free to pour BBs and hold the mag in place, making it easier to fill.

This is a great addition to any airsofter who runs M4 Magazines. Available in a range of colours to suit any loadout.

The BB funnel has been tried and tested during games, and no problems whatsoever!

It is processed from the starch of plants such as corn, sugar cane and sugar beet, making it environmentally friendly and sustainable.
