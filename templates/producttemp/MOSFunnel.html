Description
Reloading a 40MM airsoft shell can be time-consuming, especially if you have a high-capacity one. That's where this comes in. A simple design that allows you to pour BBs into your shell and quickly and effort load every chamber. No more loading it by hand or with a speed loader. This method is by far the quickest. This is a great addition to those airsofters who run grenade launchers.

How to use: Simply pour your BBs into the funnel, shake a little until all BBs have been seated into a chamber, and using a thin stick, like the cleaning rods that come with all airsoft guns, push down the BBs, pour the remanding out and you're done!

This Funnel will work with almost all 40MM Shells, like the Nuprol MOSCART / Shower Grenade and the Double Bell Grenade. The only exception to this is the hellfire grenades.

The BB funnel has been tried and tested during games, and no problems whatsoever! Still not convinced?
