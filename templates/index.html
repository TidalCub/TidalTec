Who we are
Here at Tidal Tech, we like to think of new and initiative ways to make our airsoft lives better and easier, on the field and in the safe zones for everyone. We know airsoft can be an expensive hobby, and it seems that you need to spend a lot of money to get decent gear. But we think it could be diffrent . So we have set out to create high quality but affordable asserroies and gadgets to put you ahead in the game. We believe airsoft should be more accessible and affordable, so more people can come and enjoy our hobby.

We design and manufacture accessories for airsoft, putting our years of knowledge and experience into our hobby. Check out some of our latest product, and if we don't have what you need / want. We can work with the community and come up with accessories that benefit you
Our Plan
